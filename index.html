FAQS

How does the QR Ordering System work?

The QR Ordering System allows customers to order food from the restaurant by simply scanning a QR code with their smartphone. The system is compatible with all smartphones and the order is placed directly on the restaurant's system.

Is the system compatible with all smartphones?

Yes, the system is compatible with all smartphones running iOS and Android operating systems. The system is designed to be user-friendly and easy to use.

What payment methods are accepted?

Our restaurant accepts credit/debit cards, cash, and online payment methods such as PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Wallet, and more.
